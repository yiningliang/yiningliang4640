college2=college[['COSTT4_A','SATVRMID','PCTFLOAN','TUITIONFEE_OUT','TUITIONFEE_IN']]
# We chose five elements in "Collegescore card" to determine and analyze the information we want to know. 
#"COSTT4_A" is the average cost of attendance (academic year institutions) We 
#“SATVRMID” is the midpoint of SAT scores at the institution (critical reading). We 
#“PCTFLOAN” is the percent of all federal undergraduate students receiving a federal student loan
#"TUITIONONFEE_OUT" & "TUITIONFEE_IN" stand for the tuition that students(for 'outsate'&'instate') are going to pay. 
# We believe that all five elements listed above might affect the graduation rate for underpriveleged populations.
# For example, a lower tuition rate might be more acceptable and afforable for underpriveleged populations. A higher score of SAT might demonstate that this student have more money on getting a tutor for his or her study. Offering student loans might also help student of unprivileged population be able to afford their tuition and continue studying until they graduate.
